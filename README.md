🍳 RecipeRAG AI – Intelligent Document Q&A Recipe Generator

RecipeRAG AI is an intelligent Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) application that transforms unstructured recipe documents into an interactive AI cooking assistant. Built with IBM Langflow, IBM Orchestrate, and IBM Granite Models, it enables users to upload cookbooks, PDFs, blogs, and personal recipe notes, then ask natural language questions to receive accurate, context-aware, and personalized cooking guidance.

The platform retrieves relevant recipes from a searchable knowledge base, adapts them based on dietary restrictions, allergies, available ingredients, cooking time, and cuisine preferences, and provides step-by-step cooking instructions, ingredient substitutions, nutrition facts, and automatically generated shopping lists.

✨ Key Features
📄 Upload and index recipe documents (PDFs, cookbooks, blogs)
🔍 AI-powered semantic recipe search with RAG
💬 Conversational Recipe Q&A
🍳 Personalized recipe generation
🥗 Smart ingredient substitutions
📊 Nutrition analysis and calorie insights
🛒 Automatic shopping list generation
👨‍🍳 Step-by-step cooking guidance
🤖 Multi-Agent AI powered by IBM Orchestrate & Granite Models

RecipeRAG AI redefines recipe discovery by combining RAG, Agentic AI, and IBM Foundation Models to deliver a smarter, faster, and more personalized cooking experience. 🚀🍽️
