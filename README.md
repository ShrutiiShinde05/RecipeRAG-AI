🍳 RecipeRAG AI – Intelligent RAG-Powered Recipe Assistant

RecipeRAG AI is a next-generation Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) application that converts unstructured recipe documents into an intelligent AI cooking assistant. Using IBM Langflow, IBM Orchestrate, and IBM Granite Foundation Models, the platform understands recipe documents, retrieves the most relevant cooking knowledge, and delivers personalized, context-aware culinary assistance through natural language conversations.

Whether you're uploading cookbooks, recipe PDFs, food blogs, or personal cooking notes, RecipeRAG AI enables users to discover recipes, adapt meals based on dietary preferences and available ingredients, receive nutrition insights, generate shopping lists, and follow interactive step-by-step cooking instructions—all powered by Agentic AI.

🌟 Why RecipeRAG AI?
